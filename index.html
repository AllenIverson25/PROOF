Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI verify my habits? ‚ñº
The AI analyzes your photo submissions for object presence, environmental context, and progression. It checks if gym equipment is actually in use, if your study materials are visible, if your phone is truly away. It builds a Personal Difficulty Profile based on your baseline survey and early submissions, ensuring high-effort actions get more points than easy habits.
Is my photo data private and safe? ‚ñº
Yes. Photos are analyzed by AI and then deleted within 24 hours. We don't store your images long-term. Only metadata (points, timestamps) is retained for leaderboards. Your photos are never shared with other users or third parties. We take privacy seriously.
How does personal difficulty calibration work? ‚ñº
After completing the baseline survey, PROOF tracks your first 3-5 days of submissions to build your Personal Difficulty Profile (PDP). The AI learns what's easy vs. hard for YOU specifically. A gym regular gets fewer points for a routine workout than someone who rarely exercises. This prevents "comfort farming" and ensures everyone is challenged appropriately.
What are stretch tasks? ‚ñº
Stretch tasks are optional, personalized challenges that appear mid-competition. Based on your PDP and current performance, the AI suggests high-value activities slightly beyond your comfort zone. Complete them for bonus points. Example: if you usually study 30 minutes, a stretch task might challenge you to do 60 minutes for 2x points.
What are diminishing returns? ‚ñº
Repeating the same habit earns fewer points over time within a match. If you submit gym photos 5 days in a row, the 5th earns less than the 1st. This forces habit diversity and prevents gaming the system with one easy repeated action. Keeps gameplay dynamic and encourages well-rounded growth.
How do you prevent cheating? ‚ñº
AI detects recycled photos, staged setups, and suspicious patterns. It analyzes metadata, context, and progression. If something seems off, the submission is flagged for manual review. Repeated violations result in account suspension. We're serious about fair play.
What are the safety & loss protections? ‚ñº
We cap losses: no more than $50/week for free users, unlimited for PRO subscribers who opt in. Cooldown periods between high-stakes rooms. Clear warnings before joining. PRO subscriptions offer risk-free competition modes. We prioritize healthy gameplay over extractive monetization.
How do payouts work? ‚ñº
Winner-takes-all or top X% split, depending on room type. Platform takes a flat 5-10% fee (disclosed upfront). Winnings deposited instantly after match ends via Stripe/PayPal. PRO subscribers get reduced fees and bonus multipliers. No hidden charges.
Can I play with friends? ‚ñº
Yes! Private rooms launch in V2 (Q2 2026). Set custom rules, invite friends, compete against your accountability partners. PRO subscribers can host unlimited private rooms. Great for teams, study groups, or competitive friend circles.
What's included in PRO subscription? ‚ñº
PRO users get: reduced platform fees, bonus point multipliers, advanced analytics dashboard, early access to new rooms, unlimited private room hosting, no weekly loss caps (opt-in), exclusive badges, and priority support. $9.99/month or $89.99/year.
